Meet your photographer

Documenting your best days since 2012

Hi there, I’m Viri, a photographer with a deep love for capturing the moments that matter most. With years of experience behind the lens, I’ve honed my craft to not only produce beautiful, high-quality photos, but also to create an experience that’s warm, comfortable, and, above all, feels authentic to you. I know photoshoots can be stressful at times. My goal is to make you feel at ease, so we can make magic together. I approach every session with intention, a keen eye for detail, and a focus on emotion.

So if you’re looking for more than just a photographer—someone who will truly understand the heart of what you're looking to preserve—let’s connect! I’d love to capture your story. I am a passionate photographer with over 10 years of experience in capturing life's most precious moments. My work is a blend of art and emotion, aiming to tell stories through images that evoke feelings and memories.
